**1. Axiata**
A fusion of "axiom" (fundamental legal principle) and "data," suggesting fundamental intelligence applied to legal analysis. The name is brandable, internationally pronounceable, and scalable beyond contract review into broader legal AI.

**2. Veridex**
Combining "veritas" (Latin for truth) and "index," positioning the company as a truth-finding intelligence system. The name suggests comprehensive analysis and discovery, with strong trademark potential and enterprise appeal.

**3. Cognex Legal**
Blending "cognition" and "nexus," implying intelligent connections and insights. The .legal domain reinforces industry focus while maintaining flexibility for expansion into other legal AI applications.

**4. Luminate AI**
Derived from "illuminate," suggesting the clarification and insight the platform brings to complex contracts. Clean, modern branding with clear AI positioning and room for growth across legal analytics.

**5. Praxent**
A portmanteau of "praxis" (practice of law) and "intelligent," creating a distinctive invented word. Highly brandable with no descriptive limitations, allowing for unlimited expansion while maintaining legal industry relevance through the classical legal terminology root.
